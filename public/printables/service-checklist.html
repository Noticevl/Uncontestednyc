Service & filing checklist

  • Confirm proper service method under CPLR 308
  • Process server affidavit
  • E‑filing or EDDS labels correct
  • Notarizations/RON where accepted
  • Download stamped copies for your records
  • Names and contact info for both spouses
  • Marriage date/place; separation date (if any)
  • Children’s names/DOB (if any)
  • Summary of assets/debts and who keeps what
  • Any support amounts discussed
