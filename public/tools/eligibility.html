Eligibility Quiz (Residency & Uncontested Fit)

Answer three quick questions to see if you’re likely a fit for a truly uncontested NYC divorce.

Residency — Have you or your spouse lived in NY for two years, or for one year with a NY tie (married in NY, lived in NY as spouses, or grounds in NY)?

 

Agreement — Do you both agree on all issues (property, parenting, support) or expect a true default?

 

Cooperation — Will your spouse sign or allow service/default without games?
