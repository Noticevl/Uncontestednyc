Uncontested Divorce Checklist

  • Government ID (both parties)
  • Marriage certificate (certified, if available)
  • Addresses for both spouses for service
  • Agreement on property & support (bullet outline is fine)
  • For cases with children: details on custody, parenting time, child support
  • Any prior orders (Family Court, protection orders) if relevant
