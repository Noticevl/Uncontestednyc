Frequently asked questions

Do I have to appear in court?
Most uncontested matters are resolved on papers; physical appearances are uncommon. If a hearing is scheduled, I’ll explain why and prepare you.
How long does this take?
Typical range is 8–16 weeks after signatures. Timing varies by borough workload and holidays. You’ll get realistic expectations at intake.
