Frequently asked questions

Tap a question to expand.

What does “truly uncontested” mean?

Both spouses agree on all issues (property, support, parenting) and the other spouse is cooperative (or will default).

What fees are included in the all‑in price?

Standard NY filing fees (index number, RJI, Note of Issue) and routine NYC personal service. Unusual costs (publication, international service, extensive certified copies) are not typical and quoted separately.

Do I have to appear in court?

Most uncontested cases are decided on papers. If a court requests clarification, we’ll guide you.

What if my spouse can’t be found?

We’ll discuss alternate service options (such as publication). Those are outside the flat‑fee and quoted separately.

Can I get my name changed?

Yes, we can include name change language in the judgment package.

How long does this take?

With prompt responses and clean paperwork, many clients see judgment within a few months of filing. Judicial review time varies by borough.

Will you work with my mediator or counselor?

Yes. If you have a mediated agreement, we review and convert it into the court‑ready forms.

Is this available outside NYC?

Yes, statewide, but standard service outside NYC may incur non‑standard costs. We’ll advise first.

What happens if things turn contested?

We’ll discuss options and referrals. The flat‑fee applies to uncontested matters only.

How do refunds work?

You can request a refund before we begin work on the next stage. Earned work at a prior stage is non‑refundable.
