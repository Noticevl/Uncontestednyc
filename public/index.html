Frequently asked questions

Do I have to appear in court?
Usually no. Most uncontested cases proceed entirely by e‑file/EDDS and mail. If a judge requires a brief appearance for a specific issue, we’ll explain why and handle the logistics.
How long does this take?
Many matters complete in 8–16 weeks after signatures, but timing varies by borough workload and whether the case needs special steps.
What exactly does the $5,000 include?
Attorney time through Judgment, standard filing fees (Index Number, Note of Issue equivalents), and routine NYC personal service. We provide a clear engagement letter that lists what’s included.
Are there refunds?
Yes—staged work with refunds available before each stage begins. Stage 1 refund before drafting starts; Stage 2 refund before filing/service; Stage 3 refund before judgment submissions are prepared.
What if we have children?
Your plan must be specific and workable, covering residence, time schedule, decision‑making, and communication. We draft in plain English to reduce clerk queries.
How is child support calculated?
We use the CSSA formula with combined parental income, current caps, percentages, and add‑ons. We’ll run the math and include the required worksheets.
Do we need a settlement agreement?
If your terms go beyond the standard forms, we prepare a Stipulation/Agreement that matches your deal and the court’s expectations.
Can I keep my home address private?
Yes. We can use a business address for mail and redact apartment numbers where appropriate.
Can I restore my former name?
Yes—include it in the pleadings and Judgment for a single, permanent record.
