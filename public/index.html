Frequently asked questions

Do I have to appear in court?▾
Usually no. NYC uncontested divorces are primarily paperwork.
How long does this take?▾
Many matters complete in 8–16 weeks after signatures; borough workloads vary.
What does $5,000 include?▾
Attorney time through Judgment; standard NYC filing fees; routine local service; two revision rounds; filing and tracking.
Are there refunds?▾
Yes—before work in each stage starts (drafting, filing/service, judgment prep).
Do both of us have to live in NY?▾
No. New York’s residency statute offers multiple paths; we’ll confirm yours.
What is “uncontested”?▾
You both agree on the terms so nothing needs a judge’s decision.
Can we e‑file?▾
Yes—NYSCEF where accepted; EDDS where directed.
What if we have children?▾
We’ll write clear parenting terms: residence, schedule, decisions, holidays, travel.
How is child support calculated?▾
Under CSSA with caps and add‑ons; we prepare compliant worksheets.
Can I keep my home address private?▾
Yes—use a mailing address; omit your unit publicly when permissible.
Can I restore my former name?▾
Yes—include it in pleadings and the Judgment.
What if my spouse lives elsewhere?▾
We use the simplest lawful service: acceptance, personal service, or approved alternatives.
Is mediation required?▾
No, but it can help finalize terms; we can refer mediators.
