FAQ — clear answers before you decide

Eligibility & Grounds

What does “truly uncontested” mean?

Both spouses agree on all issues (property, support, parenting) and the other spouse cooperates or will default.

Residency under DRL §230?

Typically: 2 years in NY, or 1 year with a NY tie (married in NY, marital residence in NY, or grounds in NY).

What grounds do you use?

Usually DRL §170(7) — irretrievable breakdown for at least six months.

Out‑of‑state spouse?

Yes, papers can proceed; logistics vary.

Process & Filing

Do I have to appear in court?
