Glossary

Eligibility

Multiple residency paths under DRL §230—e.g., one spouse in NY for 2 years; or 1 year with NY ties (married here or cause arose here). We’ll confirm which applies.

Timeline

Intake → drafting → signatures → filing → clerk review → Judgment. Many finish 8–16 weeks after signatures (borough workload varies).

Settlement terms

To qualify as uncontested, you both agree on property, debts, parenting, and support—or keep those out of scope. We’ll capture it cleanly.

Name change

Restoration can be included in the pleadings so your Judgment is your one permanent document.

Cost

Flat $5,000 covers attorney time, standard fees, and routine service. Unusual out‑of‑pocket items are quoted before you decide.

Refunds

Stage‑based refunds: before work, before filing, and before judgment work begins.

Parenting plan

Plain terms: where the child lives, parenting schedule, holidays, travel, decision‑making, and communication.

Child support

CSSA formula uses combined income and a percentage by number of children, then adds/adjusts for childcare and health care.

Health coverage

Spell out who covers premiums and how COBRA or marketplace coverage will work after the divorce.

Special expenses

Extracurriculars, tutoring, or other add‑ons—address who decides and how you’ll share costs.

E‑filing

We use NYSCEF wherever accepted and EDDS where directed. You receive filing confirmations and a tidy record of everything submitted.

Service

Use the simplest lawful method: acceptance or CPLR/DRL‑compliant personal service. Court‑approved alternatives if needed.

Notarization

Ink or remote online notarization (RON) per current NY guidance. We’ll provide clear signing instructions.

Clerk review

We format to local preferences and use checklists to avoid rejections.

Close the loop

Update beneficiaries, titles, and accounts; download final Judgment for your records.

Post‑judgment changes

Minor modifications can be handled later if circumstances change; we’ll outline options.

Certified copies

Order certified copies if you need them for agencies or name changes.

Record keeping

We maintain a secure archive of your signed documents and filings.
