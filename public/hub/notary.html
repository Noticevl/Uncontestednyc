Remote Online Notarization (RON) in New York

New York allows electronic notarization under Executive Law §135‑c. Notaries must register with the NY Department of State to provide electronic notarizations. Sessions use approved audio‑video technology; the notary must be physically in New York during the notarization.

  • Electronic notaries are listed/registered with the NY Department of State.
  • RON can be used for many divorce‑related affidavits if a court accepts electronic notarization.
  • If a court or clerk requires wet ink for a specific document, we’ll arrange an in‑person notarization.

Official resources: NY Executive Law §135‑c and the NY Department of State Notary FAQ.
