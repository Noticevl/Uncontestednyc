Children: custody, parenting time, and support (CSSA)

When children are involved, a truly uncontested divorce still requires clear parenting terms and support provisions that comply with the Child Support Standards Act (CSSA). We draft in plain English and attach required summaries as needed.
Parenting terms
Legal and physical custody; schedules; holidays; decision‑making.
Child support
CSSA guidelines apply unless you opt out with compliant language. We include required notices and summaries.
Forms & addenda
SCU information forms and UCS‑111 child support summary when required.
Reference: DRL §240.
