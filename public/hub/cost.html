Cost

All‑in flat fee: $5,000. This includes attorney time, standard New York statewide court filing fees (index number, RJI, Note of Issue), and routine NYC personal service. You won’t be nickeled and dimed for the basics that every uncontested case needs.
Standard fees we cover
Index Number ($210), Request for Judicial Intervention ($95), and Note of Issue ($30 where a prior RJI exists). In New York County, uncontested matrimonial matters also carry a $125 calendar fee. We track the official schedules and receipts so you don’t have to.
Not standard (quoted if needed)
Publication or other alternate service, international service, translations, apostilles, extra certified copies, deeds, and QDROs. If one of these becomes necessary, we’ll explain why and give you a clear number before moving forward.
Hardship? Courts can waive filing fees upon proper motion for poor person relief (CPLR 1101). We’ll discuss if it fits and how to apply, including the court help resources you can read yourself.
