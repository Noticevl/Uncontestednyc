Total cost & what’s included

All‑in flat fee: $5,000. This includes attorney time, standard New York statewide court filing fees (index number, RJI, Note of Issue), and routine NYC personal service.
Standard fees we cover
Index Number ($210), Request for Judicial Intervention ($95), Note of Issue ($30; or $125 if no prior RJI), and, in New York County, the calendar fee for uncontested matrimonial ($125). We monitor official fee schedules and receipts.
References: Statewide fee schedule; New York County fee list.
What’s not standard
Publication or other alternate service, international service, translations, apostilles, extra certified copies, deeds/QDROs. If needed, you’ll get a clear quote first.
Fee waiver (poor person relief). Courts can waive filing fees upon proper motion if you qualify financially (CPLR 1101). We can explain options and forms if relevant. See the court’s fee waiver guide.
