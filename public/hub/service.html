Service of process in uncontested divorce

New York requires personal delivery of the summons to a natural person unless you get a court order for alternate service. We plan logistics and proof carefully to avoid delays.
Personal service (default)
CPLR 308(1): deliver to the person. Proof is filed promptly.
“Substitute” / “nail & mail”
Only in permitted scenarios under CPLR 308(2)–(4) with strict steps and timing.
Alternate service by order
Motion under CPLR 308(5) if personal service is impracticable; the court sets the method.
References: CPLR 308.
