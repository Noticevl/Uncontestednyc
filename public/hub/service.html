Serving your spouse — two easy paths

Easiest path: your spouse signs an Affidavit of Defendant, which says they received the papers and consent. No process server needed.

If they won’t sign, we use a licensed process server. If that’s not possible, we can ask the court for permission to serve a different way.

This hub is plain‑English help for people finishing an uncontested divorce in NYC. It isn’t legal advice, but it should make the path feel less confusing.
