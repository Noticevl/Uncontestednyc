Service of Process

Service must comply strictly with CPLR requirements. Personal service by a non‑party over 18 within 120 days of filing is the default. Alternative service needs court permission.

In uncontested matters, many spouses will sign an Affidavit of Defendant acknowledging service and consenting to relief—when that happens, formal service can be unnecessary.
