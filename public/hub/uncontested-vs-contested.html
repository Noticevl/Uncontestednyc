Uncontested vs. Contested

If any material issue is disputed, the case is not uncontested and a flat‑fee uncontested service is not appropriate.

When parties are close but not fully aligned, counsel may suggest limited‑scope document review or referral to mediation to reach a complete settlement before filing.
