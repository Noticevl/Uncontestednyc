Property & debt — keep it clear

List what each of you keeps, who pays which debts, and the deadlines for any transfers. Clarity now prevents headaches later.

If there are retirement accounts, a special order (like a QDRO) might be needed after the divorce. We’ll flag that early so it doesn’t surprise you.

This hub is plain‑English help for people finishing an uncontested divorce in NYC. It isn’t legal advice, but it should make the path feel less confusing.
