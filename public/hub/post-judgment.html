Post‑Judgment

After the Judgment is signed, you may need certified copies for agencies or name‑change updates. The county clerk can issue them for a fee.

If a clerical error appears in the Judgment, counsel can move to settle/submit a corrected order or seek relief under CPLR 5019(a).
