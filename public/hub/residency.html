Residency

You must satisfy DRL §230. The common paths are (1) two years in NY immediately before filing; or (2) one year in NY plus a New York tie (married in NY, marital residence in NY, or grounds in NY). We document the facts and cite the correct paragraph in your pleadings.
Two‑year path
Either spouse has lived in NY for two continuous years before filing.
One‑year + NY tie
At least one spouse has lived in NY for one year, and you either married here, lived here as spouses, or your grounds occurred here.
