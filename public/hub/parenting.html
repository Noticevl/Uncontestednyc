Parenting

If you have kids, the agreement should cover decision‑making, parenting time, holidays, and travel. Plain terms beat legalese: the clerk wants clarity about who decides what, and when exchanges happen.

If life changes later, New York lets you modify terms through a separate process. For now, focus on a schedule that actually works.

This hub is plain‑English help for people finishing an uncontested divorce in NYC. It isn’t legal advice.
