Timeline & what courts control

Intake & retainer
We confirm it’s truly uncontested, check residency under DRL §230, select grounds, and outline the UD packet.
Drafting & sign‑off
We prepare agreements and forms, then collect signatures with compliant notarization (in‑person or RON).
File & serve
We purchase the index number, serve the other spouse, and track acknowledgments/affidavits.
Packet submission
We submit the uncontested packet via NYSCEF where permitted (with consent) or via EDDS/paper as directed.
Judicial review
The court reviews and may request clarifications. When ready, the case is placed on the calendar and your Judgment is signed.
After Judgment
We guide you on certified copies and practical updates (SSA/DMV, beneficiaries, titles).
Timing varies by county and calendar. We push promptly and keep you informed.
