Timeline — what to expect

Most people see a few months from signatures to Judgment. The biggest time variables are: how fast you can sign with a notary, how quickly we can serve your spouse (or get their signed acknowledgment), and how busy your county clerk is.

We’ll give you a plain checklist for each step so nothing sits on your plate longer than necessary.

This hub is plain‑English help for people finishing an uncontested divorce in NYC. It isn’t legal advice, but it should make the path feel less confusing.
