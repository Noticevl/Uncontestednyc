Timeline

Most uncontested NYC filings finish in a few months once documents are executed and filed. Timing varies by borough and by how quickly signatures and service are completed.

Typical phases: (1) intake and drafting; (2) signature and notarization; (3) filing and service; (4) post‑filing corrections if needed; (5) judgment review and entry by the court.
