Eligibility

You qualify for an uncontested divorce if you both want the divorce, you agree on the terms (money, property, and—if you have kids—custody/support), and New York has a connection to your case.

Common residency paths: one spouse living in NY for the last two years; or one year plus another tie to NY (like marrying here). Not sure? We’ll sort it out on a quick call.

This hub is plain‑English help for people finishing an uncontested divorce in NYC. It isn’t legal advice.
