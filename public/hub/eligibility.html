Eligibility

Residency can be satisfied through several routes under Domestic Relations Law § 230. Common NYC pathways include one spouse living in NY for two years before filing, or one year with the marriage taking place in NY or the grounds occurring in NY.

The case must be genuinely uncontested: both spouses agree to end the marriage and agree on property, debt, and—if applicable—custody, parenting time, and support.

If you have a written settlement agreement, it must be signed and properly acknowledged before a notary with the right acknowledgment form.
