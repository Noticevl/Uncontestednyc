NYSCEF / EDDS

NYSCEF is the statewide e‑filing platform for Supreme Court; EDDS accepts documents to be forwarded to courts that are not fully on NYSCEF or for certain document types.

NYC counties differ in what is accepted where. The filing cover sheets and working copies must match local preferences.
