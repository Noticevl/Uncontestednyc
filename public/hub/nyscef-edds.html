E‑filing — how it actually works

Many NYC divorces are filed through NYSCEF (the e‑filing website). Some items still go through EDDS. We handle the clicking so you don’t have to learn new portals.

Each county likes things a little different. Our job is to match those preferences so your filing isn’t bounced back.

This hub is plain‑English help for people finishing an uncontested divorce in NYC. It isn’t legal advice, but it should make the path feel less confusing.
