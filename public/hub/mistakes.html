Common mistakes — and how we avoid them

Most delays come from small things: a missing notary page, a date that doesn’t match, or child‑support language that isn’t complete. We use checklists and NYC‑specific templates to keep your case moving.

This hub is plain‑English help for people finishing an uncontested divorce in NYC. It isn’t legal advice, but it should make the path feel less confusing.
