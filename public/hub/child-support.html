Child Support & CSSA

If there are children under 21, child support must be addressed under the CSSA. The basic obligation is a percentage of combined parental income up to the statutory cap, allocated pro‑rata.

The agreement should include the statutory recitals and any deviations with reasons, to avoid clerk rejection.
