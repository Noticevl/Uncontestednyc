Child support — the simple version

New York uses a formula. It takes a percentage of combined income and divides it between parents based on income share. If you both want a different number, the agreement has to explain why and confirm that the guidelines were considered.

We’ll run the math with you and make sure the exact language the court expects appears in your papers.

This hub is plain‑English help for people finishing an uncontested divorce in NYC. It isn’t legal advice, but it should make the path feel less confusing.
