Forms Overview

Core uncontested documents commonly include the Summons with Notice or with Verified Complaint, the Affidavit of Defendant or Affidavit of Service, the Sworn Statement of Removal of Barriers to Remarriage (where applicable), Child Support Worksheet (if children), Findings of Fact and Conclusions of Law, and the Judgment of Divorce.

Exact forms and captions vary by county and whether the case is e‑filed via NYSCEF or submitted through EDDS for Supreme Court processing.
