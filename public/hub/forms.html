Forms — no legalese required

We prepare the divorce packet for you. That includes the document that opens the case, the agreement or stipulation that spells out your terms, and the final Judgment that ends the marriage. If you have kids, we include the child‑support language the court needs to see.

Your job: read carefully, confirm the facts, and sign in front of a notary (we’ll help you find one or use a remote option when allowed).

This hub is plain‑English help for people finishing an uncontested divorce in NYC. It isn’t legal advice, but it should make the path feel less confusing.
